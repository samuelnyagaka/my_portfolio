Practical Session Gallery

1. INTERMEDIATE SWITCHING

Did a practical aimed to understanding how an intermediate switch operates allowing control of a set of luminaires from three different points independently. An application being in long corridors lighting control. It as well equipped me with the knowledge of surface wiring using clips and PVC twin sheathed cables applied in offices and commercial areas where safety is not a risk and for neatness.

2. ONE GANG TWO-WAY SWITCHING

This practical equipped me with the knowledge on how two way switching using a one gang two way switches functions by switching on or off a set of lighting points from two different positions. An application of this is in the staircases, in bedrooms and even living rooms e.t.c. The practical demonstrated the importance of connecting lighting points in parallel rather than in series since in parallel connection each bulb receive full voltage thus all bulbs are brighter whereas in series the bulbs split and share voltage among them leading to reduced brightness.

3. RING MAIN CIRCUIT AND METERING

This practical helped understand how a ring main circuit for sockets works, correct cable sizing for different circuits by calculating the load i.e 2.5 mm¬≤ for socket outlets, 1.5 mm¬≤ for lighting points, 4.0 mm¬≤ for water heater e.t.c. As well I learnt how to use the PVC conduits for conduit wiring for enhanced safety, connecting fluorescent tube lamp, choosing the appropriate protective devices (MCBs) for different circuits by calculating the load, performing the major tests on an installation before connecting it to the mains power supply including the polarity test, insulation resistance test, continuity test, earthing test and visual inspection. Finally I learnt how to use the energy meter to cost the energy consumed by consumers.
